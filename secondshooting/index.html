About Me

I am Shubha, a creative technologist immersed in the film industry at Pixar Animation Studios, where I craft and perfect the final frames of each cinematic masterpiece. My work constantly revolves around the interplay of color, composition, and lighting to create captivating visuals that narrate compelling stories.

Photography isn't just my interest; it's my passion and obsession, influencing how I perceive and interact with the world. My deepest inspiration stems from the world of documentary-style photography and the beauty of raw, candid moments. I gravitate towards photography that mirrors the cinematic essence of films, adding a narrative layer to each image. My photo editing approach is anchored in the aesthetics of film, and I find continual inspiration in both fine art and the legacies of historical and contemporary photographers.

Intentions

My primary goals are to delve into the realm of wedding photography, gaining expertise in a diverse range of shots— candid, posed, details, establishing shots, and more— to enhance my breadth and depth as a photographer. I'm also eager to learn from your unique style and techniques, while also exploring the business aspects of wedding photography, including client relations, scheduling, and working with subjects. Ultimately, I aim to bring joy to clients by immortalizing their most cherished moments and ensuring the seamless flow of their special day.

Logistics

I am based in Emeryville, California, but I am open to travel for photography assignments. My availability primarily extends to weekends due to my weekday commitments. While compensation is negotiable, I aim to cover travel and lodging expenses as a minimum requirement, especially for distant projects.
